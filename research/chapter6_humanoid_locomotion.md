1. **Kajita, S., Kanehiro, F., Kaneko, K., Fujiwara, K., Harada, K., Yokoi, K., & Hirukawa, H. (2003). Resolved momentum control: Humanoid motion planning based on the linear and angular momentum. IEEE/RSJ International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems.**
