## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Content Not Loading
- Refresh the page (Ctrl+F5)
- Check your internet connection
- Clear browser cache
- Try a different browser

#### AI Chat Not Responding
- Check internet connection
- Wait a moment and try again
- Refresh the page
- Contact support if issue persists

#### Login Issues
- Verify your email and password
- Check if your account is verified
- Try resetting your password
- Clear browser cookies and cache

#### Accessibility Features Not Working
- Ensure JavaScript is enabled
- Check browser compatibility
- Disable browser extensions temporarily
- Try in an incognito/private window

### Performance Tips
- Use a modern, updated browser
- Ensure stable internet connection
- Close unnecessary browser tabs
- Use the latest version of the platform

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: How do I create a personalized learning path?**
A: Complete the professional background assessment in your profile, and the system will automatically generate personalized learning paths based on your background and goals.

**Q: Can I change my learning path?**
A: Yes, you can switch learning paths at any time. Your progress will be saved, and the system will adapt to your new path.

**Q: How often are content recommendations updated?**
A: Recommendations are updated in real-time based on your interactions and progress.

### AI Interaction

**Q: How intelligent is the AI assistant?**
A: The AI is trained specifically on the textbook content and can provide detailed explanations, examples, and clarifications related to the material.

**Q: Can the AI help with homework or assignments?**
A: The AI can help explain concepts and provide examples, but it won't directly solve assignments. It's designed to enhance learning, not replace it.

### Accessibility

**Q: Is the platform compatible with screen readers?**
A: Yes, the platform is fully compatible with major screen readers and follows WCAG 2.1 guidelines.

**Q: Can I customize the display settings?**
A: Yes, you can adjust text size, enable high contrast mode, and choose accessibility-friendly fonts.
